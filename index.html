Research

Our core areas of investigation

Neuroimaging of Trauma

Investigating brain structure and function alterations following traumatic stress exposure using advanced MRI techniques.

Computational Psychiatry

Applying computational models and machine learning approaches to understand and predict mental health trajectories.

Resilience Mechanisms

Identifying neurobehavioral factors that promote resilience and recovery following adversity and trauma.
